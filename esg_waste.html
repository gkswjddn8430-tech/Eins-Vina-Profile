ZERO WASTE

Circular Economy in Action

Overview

Eins Vina is dedicated to minimizing waste sent to landfills. We implement comprehensive recycling programs and optimize material usage to achieve our Zero Waste goals.

Fabric Recycling

Fabric scraps and off-cuts are segregated and sent for recycling or upcycling, ensuring valuable materials are reused.

Packaging Optimization

We reduce plastic packaging and prioritize biodegradable or recycled materials for all our product shipments.

Waste Segregation

Strict waste segregation protocols across the factory floor ensure that recyclable materials are never mixed with general waste.
