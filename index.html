About Us

Eins Vina represents the pinnacle of garment manufacturing efficientcy and quality control. Located in Ho Chi Minh City, we operate as a key strategic production hub for Sae-A Trading, the world's largest apparel exporter.

Equipped with state-of-the-art machinery and a skilled workforce, we specialize in OEM & ODM production for top global fashion brands. Our commitment to excellence ensures that every piece of clothing leaving our factory meets the highest international standards.

Core Competencies

Production Flow

Material

  • Fabric inspection
    (Minimum 10% inspection by 4-Point system.)
  • Fabric relaxation
    (Minimum 24hours with time control card and cover.)
  • Shade control
    (Inspect shade for all lots. Make a shade band and check it under Light box.)
  • Accessory
    (Inspect AQL 0.4. Comparing with original trim card.)

In-House LAB

  • Fabric : all style's each color / lot when fabric arrives in factory
  • Garment : Washing test before production meeting & during production and wear-wash test by performing customer's wearing.

Cutting

  • Record the history of paper pattern adjustment.
  • Make a report when adjustment & arrange mock-up.
  • Check marker layout, nap direction, shade, stripe match, plie height.
  • Cut panel inspection : plie match, stripe match, pattern match, torque, accuracy of cutting.
  • Check EMB /Print panel with approved standard.

Sewing

  • QC / QA perform Pilot-run.
  • In-process inspection – 10pcs sampling every operator 3 times a day with traffic light system to control measurement and visual.
  • In-line audit & 100% inspection at End-line.

Finishing

  • Instruct ironing method for each styles and train worker on the regular basis.
  • Utilize iron drawing on the table.
  • Finishing QC do 100% inspection.
  • Visual & Measurement inspection at designated area.

Packing

  • Performs Packing accuracy audit.
  • Can access only to packing employees.
  • Carton care stacked by Buyer/Style/PO.

Smart Factory System

We integrate cutting-edge technology into every step of the production process, ensuring precision, efficiency, and real-time monitoring.

Real-time production tracking and data analytics to optimize workflow and minimize downtime. Our MES connects every machine and operator for total visibility.
