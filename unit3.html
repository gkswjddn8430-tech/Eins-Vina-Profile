Overview

To secure field-centric innovation and expand our team to meet growing buyer needs, the Global ME Center relocated its headquarters to Eins Vina, Ho Chi Minh, in mid-2025. Supported by our GSD and IE departments, the Center collects best practices from our global network. We are transforming from a data analysis center into a solutions provider, delivering realistic alternatives and deep insights to drive the business forward.
