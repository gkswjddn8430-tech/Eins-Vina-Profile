Sample Development Center

Overview

Established in 2017, EDC delivers high-quality sample production at costs competitive with external outsourcing, backed by a dedicated fabric management system and direct-to-buyer logistics. Our team of highly skilled, long-term MRs possesses the versatility to handle diverse buyer requirements while accurately interpreting specific client nuances. We support comprehensive manufacturing capabilities for most garment types, ensuring both superior quality and operational efficiency.
