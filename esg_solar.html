SOLAR ENERGY

Powering Production with the Sun

Overview

Eins Vina has implemented extensive rooftop solar panel installations to reduce our reliance on fossil fuels. We harness clean, renewable energy to power our manufacturing lines.

Rooftop Arrays

Our expansive factory roofs are utilized for high-efficiency solar panel arrays, generating significant daily power.

Energy Independence

By generating our own power, we reduce grid load and ensure stable operations even during peak demand periods.

Carbon Reduction

Switching to solar energy significantly lowers our carbon footprint, aligning with global climate action goals.
